Speaker 1: That makes sense. So, to clarify, while anthrax spores are indeed resilient, our challenge lies in the balance between effective decontamination and practical application.

Speaker 2: Exactly. It's about finding a solution that is both effective and sustainable. Understanding these nuances is key to facing the challenge head-on.
