Speaker 2: And let’s not forget genetic variability. Different strains might react differently with age. Did they consider that diversity?

Speaker 1: They mentioned it briefly, but didn’t delve deeply into it. It seems like an opportunity for further research. If we could explore these variables more thoroughly, we might get clearer insights.

Speaker 2: Agreed. There’s potential here for a follow-up study that could really flesh out the truth behind these findings. I think it's worth investigating.

Speaker 1: Definitely. It’s a chance to either validate or challenge their claim with more robust data. I’ll reach out to the lab and see if we can collaborate. Interested?
