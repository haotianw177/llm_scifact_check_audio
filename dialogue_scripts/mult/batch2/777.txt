Decision-Maker: Excellent. Ensure the proposal aligns with the new safety guidelines. Once we have the data, we can decide whether to alter our current radiation protocols.

Practitioner: Absolutely, Dr. Carter. I'll get started on the draft and also consult with our safety officer to ensure compliance.
