Jane: Collaboration might be key here. We can’t afford to be insular with something of this magnitude. But what if it all leads to a dead end?

Dr. Lewis: Every experiment carries that risk, Jane. But the potential payoff could be significant. Imagine the impact on antibiotic treatments.

Jane: Alright, I'm convinced to explore it further. Let’s draft a proposal for a pilot study and see where it leads us.
