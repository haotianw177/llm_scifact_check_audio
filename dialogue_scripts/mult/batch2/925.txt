Advocate: Agreed. How about we propose a follow-up study that incorporates these nuances? It could help us achieve a clearer picture and ensure all factors are adequately considered.

Advocate: Indeed, achieving alignment in our understanding is crucial. After all, our ultimate goal is to enhance patient outcomes.
